Astarion Brickheadz

This was a quick and fun little project I did for one of my friends, where I recreated one of their favorite video game characters in LEGO Brickheadz form. I drew a lot of inspiration from SmurfinDaMushroom's design on Rebrickable. While I didnâ€™t purchase the instructions, I could tell that the external design of the torso section of my model closely resembled theirs. I would imagine, however, that the internals of the torso are different in my version, as I adapted it to fit my own style and approach.

The head, though, is where I had the most creative freedom. I started with the base design of a Brickheadz and then adapted it to fit the specific hair design of the character I was recreating. I spent some time experimenting with the structure and shape to make sure it captured the look while keeping the overall aesthetic of the Brickheadz style intact. The result is a fun, personalized version of the character that was both challenging and enjoyable to build!
