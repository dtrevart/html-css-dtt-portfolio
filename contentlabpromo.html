Content Lab Promotional Material

Content Lab is a student led graphic design club at VTSU Castleton, and I'm so grateful to my Co-President, Ruben Somda, for letting my take some level of responsibility with them. While it may have started as a formality of sorts, allowing me to stay at the Castleton campus instead of transfering back to Lyndon, I feel like this opportunity really helped me grow not just as a graphic designer, but as a worker as well. The real world experience that Content Lab has given me has been amazing, and I'm so thankful for everyone involved.

Coming into the Spring 2025 semester, we knew we wanted to brand ourselves more and push more to get people to know us and contact us. A fun little promotional thing we did was make a trading card game... or, well, just the cards really. This started off as joke, but Ruben REALLY liked the idea, so we sunk an entire Friday into making these cards.

The goal was to make these to hand out during the spring semester club fair to show people not just what we do, but what we can do for them! We also figured the cards wouldn't be the most popular, as a lot of them are references to things related to the Content Lab and graphic design program. With this in mind, we created a poster that could be scaled down into smaller handouts. I tried to keep the branding consistent with the Content Lab, using our fonts, colors, and iconography. I also made sure to make sure the biggest and most noticable part was a QR code with a clear call to action above/below it.
