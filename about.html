About Me

Hello! My name is Dominic Trevisani and I'm a graphic designer from North Hero, Vermont.

I'm a Vermont-based graphic designer and digital storyteller with a passion for branding, web design, and visual communication. For me, design is more than just making things look good - it's about creating meaning, telling stories, and crafting experiences that resonate.

With expertise in Adobe Suite, HTML/CSS, and UI/UX, I approach every project with strategy, creativity, and attention to detail. Whether I'm designing a brand identity, building a website, or crafting an animated graphic, my goal is always to create something meaningful and impactful.

When I'm not designing, I'm, well, still designing in a sense. LEGO has been a passion of mine for years, and I've recently gotten into building models and instructions in Bricklink Studio! Baking has become another huge hobby of mine recently, which while I enjoy it, it's a bit dangerous to have as many baked goods as I do around.

If my time at Castleton has taught me anything, it's that I love to collaborate. Let's connect and make something awesome!
