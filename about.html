About Me

"To craft meaningful, multi-disciplinary work that inspires, connects, and leaves a lasting impact."


Hello! My name is Dominic Trevisani, and I'm a graphic designer and digital storyteller from North Hero, Vermont.

I'm passionate about branding, web design, and visual communication. For me, design is more than just making things look good - it's about creating meaning, telling stories, and crafting experiences that resonate.

With expertise in Adobe Suite, HTML/CSS, and UI/UX design, I approach every project with strategy, creativity, and attention to detail. Whether I'm building a brand identity, designing a website, or creating animated graphics, my goal is always to craft something meaningful and impactful.

Outside of design, I'm a massive LEGO enthusiast, recently diving into building models and instructions with Bricklink Studio. I also love baking, although having a kitchen full of sweets can be a bit dangerous...

If my time at Castleton has taught me anything, it's that I thrive on collaboration. Let's connect and create something awesome together!
