Lego Minecraft Desert Well

One of the big things that got me back into LEGO, and especially LEGO Minecraft, was Cheesey Studio's LEGO Minecraft Series, where he made builds accurate to the game. I never knew that 5 2x2 LEGO plates stacked on top of each other make a cube, as I figured if there was a way to make perfect cubes that LEGO would've done it in their sets, but I guess not, probably to keep the sets affordable.

The first build in his series was a desert well, an incredibly useless structure, but a simple one. I decided to make my own build and instructions in Bricklink Studio as it didn't seem like a big project, and I really wanted to have one for myself. I added some of Cheesey Studio's Phantoms, since I think the design is perfect, and I think Phantoms are really cute, and I also added a moon, a design heavily inspired from @farawaybricks since I thought adding a moon would help set it apart from Cheesey's, as well as make adding the Phantoms easier, and allow me to add Husks!
