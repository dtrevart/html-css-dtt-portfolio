Apple Still Life

This assignment was the first time I ventured into digital painting, and I was genuinely excited about it. Although it turned out to be more of an in-class exercise than a full-fledged project, I'm thrilled with how it turned out. For my first attempt at a digital still life, I believe it closely resembles our reference image.

I approached this piece as an opportunity to practice and refine my digital painting skills. The assignment allowed me to experiment with shading, texture, and color blending, which are crucial elements in creating realistic digital artwork.

Overall, this exercise was a valuable learning experience that has inspired me to continue exploring digital painting techniques.
