Castleton Sustainability Team Earth Day Posters

Mary Droege originally came to my Posters & Promotions class, talking about some of the projects she needs graphic work done for. One of which was for the Friends of Castleton Parks & Recreations , who were in need of a new poster for their garage sale event coming up in May, as well as a poster for the Castleton Sustainability Team's Earth Day events. Since I was already working on FCP&R with Content Lab, I figured I would probably take on their poster for the class, but that all changed when I walked into FAC 109 on a Saturday to move files from an SD card to a USB.

I ran in to Bill DeForest, my advisor and professor for our Posters & Promotions class, printing out programs for Hamlet. He told me that rather than needing the posters for Earth Week around the middle of April, they actually needed it by the start of the week, giving him 2-3 days to make one. I ended up staying in the classroom, making some concepts for the poster, and tweaking it with help from Bill, until we got something we really liked with Lumpy Earth!

We sent it over to Mary, and other than a few small tweaks to the text and order of events, she loved it! While the turn around time on this was really fast, I'm really proud of what I was able to come up with in such a short amount of time!
