Omori Playing Cards

Playing OMORI was one of the most emotional and pivotal experience of my life. I won't get into it too much, but the game is about trauma, how people process it, and how to move on. I haven't ever sobbed as I hard as I have from any form media as I did with this game. It destroyed me in so many ways, so clearly I had to create something to commemorate my time with this game, and the opportunity presented itself for my Vector Graphics final project: a deck of playing cards.

It seemed almost perfect. A lot of this game revolves around the joys of childhood and how simple things were. One of the many ways this theme is communicated with the player is through the use of childrens games. An OMORI deck of cards just seemed like a no brainer to me.

The back of the cards is inspired from the sky in headspace. The 3 characters represetned on the Jack, Queen, and King are heavily inspired from the character models in game, and the same goes for the figures on the Jokers. The font used is the font used in the game, created for the game.
