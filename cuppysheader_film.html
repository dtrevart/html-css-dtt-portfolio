Cuppy's Header Film

Calling this a "film" may be a stretch, but with the camera work, angles, and editing, I think I made something really clean. For my other website, Cuppy's, I wanted to try and make something more interactive, and a big part of that for me was an animated header, something similar to what I did on this site. I figured that an actual animation might not fit super well with the brand, so I decided to enlist my girlfriend to help film me making bread! We both had a pretty good vision of what we wanted, and I really think that comes through in the final version.
