Magazine Layout

For one of my assignments for my Typography class, we were tasked with finding an article to recreate as a magazine spread. I decided to find an article about cats and use that as a starting point, and eventually I found an article that spoke to me, and with that, I created this magazine spread! I took the colors from the cat on the title page to get the pallet for this project, and I did some shape building with text a scratch vector to create the title art.
