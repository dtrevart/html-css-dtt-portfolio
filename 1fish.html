1Fish

1Fish was born out of a small multiples assignment in my Branding and Packaging class. We were challenged to create several distinct marks that still felt as if they were part of a larger whole. I wanted to design something that felt cohesive yet flexible, unified under a simple but memorable name: 1Fish. The name itself plays with the idea of singular identity amidst variation, which became the guiding principle for the visual system.


While the final look leans toward a clean, corporate aesthetic, that structure gave me room to focus on consistency in form, scale, and rhythm throughout the logo. The result is a design that feel professional and modern, connected by shape language and a shared visual tone. Though 1Dish doesn't currently exist as a real brand, the process offered a strong exercise in developing branding systems that could extend across multiple uses and applications.
