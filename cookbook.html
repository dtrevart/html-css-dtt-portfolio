Cookbook Pages

Baking has become a deep passion of mine within the last year. Since picking it up, I've often found myself misplacing or forgetting the tweaks I made to various recipes. To address this, I decided to create my own recipe pages - a personal project that allowed me to merge my love for baking with my skills in graphic design, as well as use it as an opportunity to practice photography.

Each recipe page features a treat or good I've made, accompanied by photographs I've taken myself. The recipes are adaptations of ones I've found online, modified to suit my tastes and preferences. This endeavor not only helped me keep track of my culinary experiments but also provided an avenue to practice and refine my design and photography skills.

Through this project, I've been able to create a personalized collection of recipes that reflect both my culinary journey and my growth as a designer.
