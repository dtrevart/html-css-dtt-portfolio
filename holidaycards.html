Holiday Cards

This was one of my favorite assignments from my time at Lyndon! We were tasked to make a holiday card for any random holiday. It started off with a bunch of rough thumbnails, then moving forward to choosing 3, presenting them to the class, then honing in on one of those to make a final card!
I remember sitting in my Western Music History class paying absolutely no attention to the Material being taught as I worked on these thumbnails the same day the assignment got assigned. I had plent of ideas of what I wanted to do, choosing some obscure holidays since I thought that seemed more fun. While I had silly ideas for National Scribble Day and even Apple Giving Day of all "holidays", I decided to go with graduation, Valentines day, and National Kitten Day.

Roughs

Out of these 3, the graduation card seemed to be the biggest hit. Honestly, this was my favorite too. The Valentines day one is in a bit of a different style, and is a little unsettling (though that is the point), and I really only made the National Kitten Day to bother my professor. There was a handful of feedback for the graduation card, however. The parents in the background were too big, taking away from the graduate, the graduate was at kind of a weird angle, and with the way the eyes were, you couldn't really tell that's what they were, causing confusion as to if the graduate was going to hug their parents. I also added one of those silly graduation things you get for being a good student or whatever, and overall just refined it a lot, making a much more cohesive card!
