VTSU Student Voter Drive

Rich Clark, a Political Science professor at VTSU - Castleton, came to our graphic design club, Content Lab, asking for us to help make a student voter campaign to help push students to, well, vote. Castleton had a similar campaign back during the previous presidental election, going under Sparty Votes, but since Castleton is now part of VTSU, and this voter drive is directly tied to VTSU, we would have to find a new way to reach students. Since this was a group project within our club, we all tackled different parts of it. I started messing around during one of our meetings, and people began to gather and we ended up creating our graphic standards pretty soon after, making small tweaks along the way.

We did some targeted posters towards our athletics programs, with sports and other things being highly incorporated into the design, though I didn't take part in those. Instead, I work on relatively generic and broad advertising that we gave to our Student Government to present to the other campuses. I believe they picked it up to be used across all campuses, though I'm unsure how much it got pushed.
