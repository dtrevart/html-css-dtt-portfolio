STAX Animated Logo

This animated logo project marked my first foray into motion design, and I'm still proud of how it turned out. Assigned in a class with the simple brief to create an animated logo for either a real or fictional company, I chose to invent one from scratch. While the specifics of what "Stax" does remain undefined, the focus was on exploring animation techniques and bringing a brand identity to life through motion.

The process involved conceptualizing the brand's visual identity, designing the logo, and animating it to convey a sense of dynamism and professionalism. This project not only honed my skills in animation but also deepened my understanding of how motion can enhance brand storytelling.
