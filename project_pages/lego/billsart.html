Buffalo Bills Art

This was my very first LEGO project made using Bricklink Studio, and it was created as a Christmas gift for my dad. The project itself is a simple recreation of the Buffalo Bills logo, but it holds a special place for me as my first venture into LEGO design using digital tools. While the design itself is straightforward, just a faithful representation of the logo, it marked the beginning of my journey into building custom LEGO creations and experimenting with different styles and techniques.

Although it's a simple project, it felt like the perfect way to dive into this creative process and to make something personal for my dad. The experience gave me a sense of accomplishment and set the foundation for many more complex and exciting LEGO projects to come.
