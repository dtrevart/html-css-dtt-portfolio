Sable Ward Brickheadz

This was another fun little side project where I recreated one of my favorite Dead by Daylight characters, Sable Ward, in LEGO BrickHeadz form. I’ve always been drawn to the way her character blends eerie elegance with emotional depth, and I thought she’d translate really well into this stylized format.

I followed the general BrickHeadz structure but made sure to incorporate key design elements that reflect Sable’s distinct look. Even within the simplified style, her silhouette and overall tone still shine through.

The head was especially fun to build. I spent time experimenting with different ways to capture her hairstyle and facial features while keeping everything clean and recognizable in LEGO form. The result is a spooky yet charming version of Sable Ward, brought to life in compact BrickHeadz scale.
