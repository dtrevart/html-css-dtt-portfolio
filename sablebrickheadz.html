Sable Ward Brickheadz

To much of the Dead by Daylight community’s dismay, I am (unfortunately) a Sable Ward main. And yes, I do think she’s pretty, but honestly, I started playing her because of her perks. They’re super niche, widely considered not very good, and that’s exactly what made them interesting to me. I get the most fun out of finding ways to make “bad” things work, and while calling my survivor gameplay strong might be a stretch, it’s that underdog vibe that made me really connect with Sable, and ultimately inspired me to build her in LEGO.

After doing a bit of research, I realized there aren’t many Dead by Daylight LEGO creations out there. I found one BrickHeadz version of Sable Ward, but it wasn’t quite my style. That said, it still served as a reference point as I started designing my own.

Originally, I gave her bright pink tips in her hair, mostly inspired by that earlier BrickHeadz example, but after taking a closer look at in-game references (and realizing I was just projecting what I wanted her hair to look like), I swapped them out for a few more subtle, accurate highlights. It helped clean up the model and brought it closer to her actual design.

While she’s not the most complex build I’ve done, everything came together pretty smoothly, and I’m really happy with how she turned out. She’s simple, recognizable, and, in my completely biased opinion, a fun little tribute to one of my favorite characters!
