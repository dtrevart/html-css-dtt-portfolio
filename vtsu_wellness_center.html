    • VTSU Wellness Center

    In the fall of 2024, the VTSU Wellness Center came to our class asking if we could redesign a logo and brand identity for them, as each campus had their own individual identity, but they weren't coherent of cohesive with each other. They mainly wanted it to give off wellness, as well as fit into the VTSU brand identity. From there, I created a creative brief and mood board to get a sense of the direction I wanted to go with.

    Creative Brief Pages

    While constructing the branding, there were some themes I had kept in mind. The main theme was having one main logo made up of 4 individual parts, directly relating to how VTSU is made up of 4 colleges, being Castleton University, Vermont Technical College, and Northern Vermont University, which was made up of Johnson State College and Lyndon State College. Alongside this, I wanted each campus to have their own identities represented, something I think that was lost while VTSU's identity was being created. The other themes were general wellness themes, having some figure that resembles a human, as well as having some resemblance of a heart and plants, being a clover in this case, to connect it more with growth and other similar ideas, as this theme came up quite a bit while doing research. In my creative breif, you can see that I included colors from every campus, and I ended up scrapping the Northern Vermont University colors in the end, as the school wasn't around very long, and I felt that the individual campus colors captured more identity.
